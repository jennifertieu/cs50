About Me

Hi, my name is Jennifer. I'm 23 years old and I'm from Pearland, Texas. For those who don't know, its a small city south of Houston. I graduated from the University of Texas at Austin with a Geography degree and a minor in Computer Science. I'm currently working as a Geographic Information System Technician for an oil and gas company in downtown Houston. It's been about 3 years since I started working and throughout that time I discovered my passion for programming. Therefore, I wanted to learn more and signed up for CS50. My experience with the course so far has been amazing! I've learned more than I thought I would and I have been challenged every step of the way. This is the first website I am building and it will hopefully not be my last. Cheers to the instructor, David J. Malan, and the CS50 staff!
